Getting Here

Choose Your Route

Whether you're driving, taking a train, or flying in — we've got directions for every traveller.

Most Popular

By Road

Delhi → Stone Age Huts · ~300 km

Approximately 300 km from Delhi — about 7–8 hours via NH9 and local mountain roads. From Nainital city it's a scenic 1.5–2 hour drive up through the pine forests.

Kathgodam is the nearest major town, around 90 km away. The road from Kathgodam winds beautifully through forested valleys.

Start from Delhi by 5–6 AM to enjoy daylight driving on the mountain roads.
Budget Friendly

By Train

Kathgodam Station · ~90 km away

Take any train to Kathgodam Railway Station — the last railhead before the hills. Trains run from Delhi, Lucknow, and Dehradun regularly.

From Kathgodam, shared jeeps and private taxis are available. The scenic drive to the resort takes roughly 2 hours through forested roads.

Try the Ranikhet Express or Shatabdi from Delhi for comfortable travel.
Fastest

By Air

Pantnagar Airport · ~80 km away

Pantnagar Airport (PGH) is the closest airport, about 80 km from the resort. Regular flights operate from Delhi with a journey time under 1 hour.

Hire a cab at the airport or pre-book a transfer directly to the resort. The drive through the foothills takes approximately 2.5 hours.

Contact us to pre-arrange a pickup from Pantnagar airport.
Step by Step

Delhi to Stone Age Huts

A simple 4-step guide for the most common route from Delhi.

1

Start from Delhi

Take NH9 (Noida Expressway) towards Moradabad. Leave early morning to beat traffic.

2

Reach Haldwani

Drive via Moradabad and Rampur to Haldwani. Refuel and rest here (~5 hrs from Delhi).

3

Via Kathgodam

Continue to Kathgodam, then take the mountain road towards Pangot and Nainital hills.

4

Arrive at Resort

Follow GPS to "Stone Age Huts, Tushrpani, near Pangot, Nainital". Welcome! 🎉

On the Ground

Local Transport & Landmarks

Once you're in the region, here's how to get to us and what to watch for.

🚕

Local Taxis

Private taxis from Nainital bus stand and Kathgodam station at fixed rates. Negotiate beforehand.

🚐

Shared Jeeps

Budget-friendly shared jeeps from Kathgodam and Nainital towards Pangot. Ask for Tushrpani route.

🏛️

Naina Devi Temple

A famous nearby landmark. Tell your driver to head past Naina Devi temple towards the Pangot road.

📍

Village of Tushrpani

Our resort is in Tushrpani, Bagar Malla. Ask locals for "Stone Age Huts near Pangot Bird Sanctuary."

🏍️

Bike Rentals

Motorbike rentals available in Nainital for adventurous travellers who prefer riding up the hills.

📞

Call Us for Help

Lost on the mountain road? Call us directly — we'll guide you through the final stretch to the resort.

Using GPS or Google Maps?

Our pin is accurately placed on Google Maps. Just tap Navigate and follow along — the road is well-marked from Pangot junction.
